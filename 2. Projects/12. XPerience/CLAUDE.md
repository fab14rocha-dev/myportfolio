# 12. XPerience

## What It Is
A web platform for a personalized immersive experience business. Customers book surprise experiences (sports, dining, music) tailored to each guest's preferences — without the guest knowing the details in advance.

## The Business
- Owner: Joseph (last name TBD after call)
- Business concept: 60-minute curated experiences personalized around a guest's favorite sport, song, food, or life moments
- Examples of experiences:
  - Dining — personalized menu, red carpet restaurant entry by name, handwritten card
  - Sports — e.g., NY Knicks court experience, scoreboard moment, shooting challenge
  - Music — private concert featuring the guest's favorite songs
- Related sites to reference:
  - https://josephlobello.primespotsolutions.multisiteadmin.com/programs
  - www.ExperienceGreenville.com
- AI company of interest: Songer (explore how it could be integrated as a surprise element)

## The Three Core Problems to Solve
1. **Discreet data collection** — Capture guest preferences (favorite sport, song, food, memories) in a storytelling or conversational format without tipping them off that it's being used to plan their surprise
2. **Sales without spoilers** — Show potential customers what they're buying without revealing what the actual surprise looks like
3. **AI personalization** — Use AI to take collected info and personalize the experience details (menus, scoreboard names, song selections, etc.)
