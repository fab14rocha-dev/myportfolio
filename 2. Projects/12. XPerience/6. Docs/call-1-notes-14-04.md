# Call 1 — Discovery Call
**Date:** 14 April 2026

## Key Takeaways

1. **Form style** — Joseph wants a conversational form, not a standard boring form. He found a tool he liked: [involve.me](https://www.involve.me/) as a reference for the feel he's going for.

2. **Willing to spend on Ads** — He's open to paid ads to test the idea. Budget not confirmed yet.

3. **Target audience agreed** — Ages 21–45, focused on birthdays and wedding anniversaries.

4. **Pricing tiers (3 levels, per couple):**
   - **Tier 1 — $500** — 60 min, choose one experience (sports / dining / music), includes a personalised song (via Songer AI)
   - **Tier 2 — $1,200** — Two experiences combined (e.g. dining + music)
   - **Tier 3 — $2,500** — ~3 hour experience, all three experiences combined, full personalisation

5. **Songer** — The AI tool Joseph mentioned for generating personalised songs. Worth researching before next call.

6. **Two intake flows identified:**
   - **Flow A — Couple surprising each other** — Questions need to be discreet, masked so neither person knows what the other is answering
   - **Flow B — One person surprising another** — Straightforward, the buyer fills in the guest's preferences

7. **Action items from call:**

   - Fabricio to build a landing page focused on wedding anniversaries with the intake flow
