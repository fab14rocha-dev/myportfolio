What I Offer

Calligraphy and engraving for luxury brands and corporate events.

I work with premium brands and events teams across the UK, bringing live personalisation to brand activations, product launches, and corporate gifting. Every piece is made by hand, in front of your guests — a moment of craft that turns a standard event gift into something genuinely remembered.

01

Engraving

Engraving is the art of personalising items in real time, at your event or activation. Using a rotary engraver, I personalise glass, metal, leather, and more — in front of your guests.

From perfume bottles and watches to crystal glasses and leather accessories, each piece becomes a unique keepsake that guests take home long after the event ends.

From £90/hr  ·  2-hour minimum

Calligraphy

Calligraphy brings the art of hand lettering directly to your event. I write on cards, menus, envelopes, branded packaging, and more — creating something personal and memorable for every guest.

The experience of watching a skilled calligrapher at work is something guests genuinely stop for. It draws people in and gives your event a moment of real craft.

Pricing on request  ·  In-studio & on-site
