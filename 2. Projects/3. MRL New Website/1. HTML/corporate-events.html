Corporate Events

The Experience

Personalisation that guests remember.

Corporate events need to leave an impression. Engraving and calligraphy transform a standard branded gift into something guests genuinely treasure. The act of watching their item personalised in front of them is a moment they will share.

I work with brand teams, event agencies, and in-house events managers to deliver a seamless live experience. I understand the detail, the timing, and the standards that luxury and corporate events demand.

How It Works

Simple to brief, seamless to deliver.

01

Brief

Share your event details — date, location, number of guests, the items to be personalised, and any brand guidelines. I will confirm feasibility and send a quote.

02

Prepare

I prepare all equipment and, if needed, test on sample items in advance. I work with you to plan the setup so it fits naturally into the event flow.

03

Deliver

On the day, I arrive ahead of time, set up discreetly, and deliver a polished, professional experience. I handle the queue, the guests, and the craft — you focus on the rest of the event.

Common Questions

Can you offer both engraving and calligraphy at the same event? +

Yes — combining both services is popular for larger activations. I can run both stations simultaneously, or switch between services depending on the event structure. Get in touch to discuss the format.

How do you handle large queues of guests? +

I work efficiently without rushing. For very high-volume events, I can advise on a queue management approach or extend the event duration to ensure everyone is served comfortably.

What do you need from us in advance? +

Access to a small table and a power outlet is usually all that's required for engraving. Calligraphy needs no power. I will confirm the setup requirements when I send your quote.

Can you work at outdoor events? +

Yes, with a suitable table and some shelter from wind and direct sunlight. I will advise on what works for the specific outdoor setup you have in mind.
