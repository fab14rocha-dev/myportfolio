Calligrapher & Engraver

Every letter tells a story.

I'm a professional calligrapher and engraver based in Leeds, Yorkshire. For the past five years I've dedicated myself to the art of hand lettering, working with some of the world's most recognised luxury brands.

My work sits at the intersection of craft and performance. Whether engraving a perfume bottle at a brand activation or writing place cards for an exclusive dinner, I bring the same quiet precision to every single piece.

The Journey

How it all started.

My passion for calligraphy began whilst I was on maternity leave with my second son. I was immediately drawn to the beauty and elegance of calligraphy, and with time, what started as a personal practice grew into a career working alongside brands that share my commitment to quality and detail.

I've worked with Vacheron Constantin, Dior, Maison Francis Kurkdjian, Ted Baker, Harvey Nichols, Patrón, Carmex, Channel 4, and many more — each collaboration building on the last, each one demanding something slightly different.

Based in Leeds and available nationwide, I travel across the UK for brand activations, corporate events, and private commissions. My work has been featured in Save The Date and Yorkshire Guide.

Events

The experience is part of the art.

Live work is where I thrive. There is something about creating in front of an audience that raises the stakes — and I love it. Guests gather, watch, and leave with something especially made for them at that moment.

That is the value brands come back for. Not just a personalised product, but a memory. A reason to stop, engage, and share. Engraving and calligraphy turn a good event into one people talk about.
