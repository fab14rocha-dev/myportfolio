How Live Calligraphy Made This Vacheron Constantin Event Extra Special

Earlier this year, Vacheron Constantin hosted a private event at their Leeds boutique for their valued clients, and I had the privilege to add a special touch with my live calligraphy.

I was there to personalise Louis Roederer champagne gift boxes. The personalisation happened live, allowing guests to decide what they'd like on their gift box and then watch it being created with my favourite gold ink.

Receiving a champagne gift box is already an amazing experience, but imagine the impact of having it personalised live in front of your clients, with their own message. This unique service brings the whole experience to the next level, making it truly special.

Vacheron Constantin's commitment to quality and precision has made them a symbol of excellence, and I was so honoured to work with them — and I still am, as we now regularly work together.

If you'd also like to add calligraphy or engraving to your event, get in touch. We can discuss your vision, event details and personalisation options.
