Engraving

The Craft

Made for them, right in front of them.

Engraving is the art of personalising items in real time, at your event or brand activation. Using a hand engraving machine, I inscribe names, initials, dates, and messages onto a wide range of surfaces.

Guests watch their item transformed — in minutes — into something made specifically for them. The result is a keepsake that carries far more meaning than any off-the-shelf gift.

I have engraved for some of the world's most prestigious brands, including Vacheron Constantin, Dior, Maison Francis Kurkdjian, and Ted Baker.

What Can Be Engraved

Almost anything with a hard surface.

  • Glass — perfume bottles, candles, and glasses
  • Plastic — beauty products such as lipsticks, lip glosses, and compact mirrors

Perfect For

Events that demand something more.

  • Brand activations and product launches
  • Corporate gifting and end-of-year events
  • Retail pop-ups and VIP shopping experiences

I engrave across a wide range of materials and work at events of every scale — from intimate retail pop-ups to large brand activations. If you have something specific in mind, get in touch and I will confirm what's possible and what to expect on the day.

Common Questions

How long does each engraving take? +

Most pieces take between 3 and 8 minutes depending on the amount of text and complexity of the design. This means I can comfortably serve a large number of guests over a 2-3 hour activation.

Do you travel outside Leeds? +

Yes — I am available nationwide across the UK. Travel costs are discussed as part of the quote process and are usually straightforward to factor in.

Can you use our brand's font or logo? +

No. I am unable to engrave logos or replicate computer fonts.

Do you supply the items to be engraved? +

No, I focus on the engraving itself. Items are typically supplied by the brand or event organiser. I am happy to advise on what works well for engraving if you are still choosing products.
