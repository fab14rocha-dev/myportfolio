Live Engraving for Events: What You Need to Know

Planning an event is all about creating memorable experiences that leave a lasting impression on your guests. One trend that has been gaining momentum, especially in luxury and high-end events, is live engraving.

What is Live Engraving?

Live engraving is the art of personalising items on-site during an event. Using specialised tools, I can customise materials like glass, metal or plastic with names, dates, or meaningful messages. This transforms ordinary objects into extraordinary keepsakes that guests cherish.

Why Choose Live Engraving for Your Event?

Unique and Personalised Experience

Every guest receives something made specifically for them. A name, a date, a short message — it turns a standard gift into something genuinely personal. That level of bespoke attention is what guests remember long after the event ends.

Memorable Entertainment

The process of live engraving is mesmerising to watch. Guests are drawn in, they film it, they share it. It creates a natural gathering point at any event and generates organic content that extends your brand's reach.

Elevated Brand Activation

For brands, live engraving communicates artistry, quality, and attention to detail — values that resonate with luxury and corporate audiences alike. It turns a product moment into a brand experience.

Types of Events Perfect for Live Engraving

  • Corporate Events — engraving branded giveaways like flasks and spirits
  • Weddings — personalising wedding favours or keepsakes for guests
  • Luxury Brand Activations — customising perfume bottles and beauty items
  • PR Events — adding a unique touch to gifts

How to Incorporate Live Engraving into Your Event

Choose the right engraver. Look for someone with a relevant portfolio and experience working with the type of items you have in mind. Not every engraver has worked on curved surfaces, delicate packaging, or high-volume events — so it is worth asking.

Decide on the items. Glass, metal, and plastic are all workable materials. Consider what will feel most meaningful to your guests and what fits your brand or occasion best.

Plan ahead. The earlier you get in touch, the smoother the logistics. I can advise on setup requirements, timing per item, and how to structure the queue so the experience flows well on the day.

Common Questions

How much does live engraving cost? Costs vary based on location, event duration, materials, and the complexity of the personalisation. I work on an hourly rate — get in touch with your brief and I will come back with a quote.

How long does each item take? Most items take between two and five minutes, depending on the material and what is being engraved. I can give a more accurate estimate once I know what you have in mind.

What materials can be engraved? Glass, metal, and plastic are all options. Some materials require more care than others — for example, engraving on curved surfaces like bottles or tins demands a different technique to flat items.

If you are considering live engraving for your next event, get in touch. I would love to hear about your brief and talk through how it could work.
