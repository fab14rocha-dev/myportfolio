Calligraphy

The Art

Writing that stops people in their tracks.

Calligraphy is exactly what it sounds like: me writing, in front of your guests, creating something personal and beautiful in real time. On cards, envelopes, menus, product packaging, or directly on bottles and boxes.

The act of watching a skilled calligrapher at work creates a moment guests genuinely pause for. It adds theatre, warmth, and craft to any event — and the result is something they take home and keep.

What I Write On

Paper, packaging, and everything in between.

  • Cards — place cards, gift tags, thank you notes, name cards
  • Envelopes — hand-addressed invitations and correspondence
  • Branded stationery and event programmes
  • Bottles and jars — wine, spirits, fragrance, skincare

Common Questions

How many guests can you serve in one event? +

It depends on the complexity of each piece. Simple name cards or short inscriptions can be completed quickly, serving a high volume of guests. Longer or more elaborate pieces take more time. I will advise based on your specific needs.

Can guests choose what they want written? +

Absolutely. Most activations involve guests choosing a name, short message, or phrase. I am happy to work to a defined list of options or allow guests free choice, depending on what works best for your event.

What ink or materials do you use? +

I use professional-grade calligraphy inks and dip pens. For non-paper surfaces, I use specialist markers and pens suited to the material. Everything is food-safe where required and will not smear or fade.

Can you work from a specific style or script? +

Yes — I have a wide repertoire of scripts and styles. If your brand has a specific aesthetic, share examples when you get in touch and I will confirm whether it can be matched or adapted.

Do you travel outside Leeds? +

Yes, I am available nationwide. Travel costs are included in the quote and are usually simple to accommodate.
