Design 10 — The Complete

The homepage was built through 7 design explorations (designs 10–16, all in designs/). Design 10 was chosen. It lives in index.html and is the direction we're going with. The others are reference only.

Copy Notes

Voice & tone

  • Professional but warm — Martina is the face of the brand, it should feel personal
  • Minimal and direct — no filler copy, every sentence earns its place
  • Elegant phrasing — words like "crafted", "bespoke", "refined" fit the brand
  • First person where appropriate — she speaks directly to her audience
