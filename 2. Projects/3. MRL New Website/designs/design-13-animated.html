About Martina

Professional calligrapher and engraver based in Leeds.

I opened my calligraphy studio in 2022. Over time, I realised I wanted to offer fewer services, of outstanding quality, and that's when I started to specialise in traditional calligraphy and engraving.

I've had the privilege of working with amazing world-renowned brands such as Dior, Vacheron Constantin, Channel 4, and Maison Francis Kurkdjian.

My style is elegant and minimalistic — I believe less is more. My business is my greatest passion.
