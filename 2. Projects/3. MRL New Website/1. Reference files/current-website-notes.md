## Services (3 main)

### 1. Live Engraving & Brand Activations
- Done on-site at brand events: fragrances, beauty products, wine, spirits
- Also available as in-studio service
- Occasions: Christmas, Mother's Day, Valentine's Day, corporate events, private events
- **Pricing:** from £90/hour, 2-hour minimum, travel costs may apply

### 2. Live Calligraphy
- For in-store brand events
- Done on paper (branded note cards) or directly on products (e.g. champagne gift boxes)
- Customers stop, watch, share online — adds brand visibility
- On-site and in-studio

### 3. Wedding & Event Calligraphy
- Place cards, envelopes, stationery
- Limited number of wedding clients per year (quality over quantity)
- High quality, regular updates, total commitment
