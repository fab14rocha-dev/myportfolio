Current Text

Q1How many guests can you serve in one event?

A: It depends on the complexity of each piece. Simple name cards or short inscriptions can be completed quickly, serving a high volume of guests. Longer or more elaborate pieces take more time. I will advise based on your specific needs.

 

Q2Can guests choose what they want written?

A: Absolutely. Most activations involve guests choosing a name, short message, or phrase. I am happy to work to a defined list of options or allow guests free choice, depending on what works best for your event.
