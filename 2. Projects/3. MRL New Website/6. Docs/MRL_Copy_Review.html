FAQs (Engraving)

Current TextMartina's Version (leave blank if OK)
Q1How long does each engraving take?
A: Most pieces take between 3 and 8 minutes depending on the amount of text and complexity of the design. This means I can comfortably serve a large number of guests over a 2-3 hour activation.
Q2Do you travel outside Leeds?
A: Yes — I am available nationwide across the UK. Travel costs are discussed as part of the quote process and are usually straightforward to factor in.
Q3Can you use our brand's font or logo?
A: Yes. I can work from brand guidelines and incorporate specific fonts, logos, or motifs where the engraving process allows. Please share any brand assets when you get in touch.
Q4What if a guest makes a mistake in their request?
A: I always confirm the exact text with the guest before engraving begins, so there is very little room for error. In the rare case of an issue, I will discuss options with you on the day.
Q5Do you supply the items to be engraved?
A: I focus on the engraving itself. Items are typically supplied by the brand or event organiser. I am happy to advise on what works well for engraving if you are still choosing products.

FAQs (Calligraphy)

Current TextMartina's Version (leave blank if OK)
Q1How many guests can you serve in one event?
A: It depends on the complexity of each piece. Simple name cards or short inscriptions can be completed quickly, serving a high volume of guests. Longer or more elaborate pieces take more time. I will advise based on your specific needs.
Q2Can guests choose what they want written?
A: Absolutely. Most activations involve guests choosing a name, short message, or phrase. I am happy to work to a defined list of options or allow guests free choice, depending on what works best for your event.
Q3What ink or materials do you use?
A: I use professional-grade calligraphy inks and dip pens. For non-paper surfaces, I use specialist markers and pens suited to the material. Everything is food-safe where required and will not smear or fade.
Q4Can you work from a specific style or script?
A: Yes — I have a wide repertoire of scripts and styles. If your brand has a specific aesthetic, share examples when you get in touch and I will confirm whether it can be matched or adapted.
Q5Do you travel outside Leeds?
A: Yes, I am available nationwide. Travel costs are included in the quote and are usually simple to accommodate.

FAQs (Corporate Events)

Current TextMartina's Version (leave blank if OK)
Q1Can you offer both engraving and calligraphy at the same event?
A: Yes — combining both services is popular for larger activations. I can run both stations simultaneously, or switch between services depending on the event structure. Get in touch to discuss the format.
Q2How do you handle large queues of guests?
A: I work efficiently without rushing. For very high-volume events, I can advise on a queue management approach or extend the event duration to ensure everyone is served comfortably.
Q3What do you need from us in advance?
A: Access to a small table and a power outlet is usually all that's required for engraving. Calligraphy needs no power. I will confirm the setup requirements when I send your quote.
Q4Can you work at outdoor events?
A: Yes, with a suitable table and some shelter from wind and direct sunlight. I will advise on what works for the specific outdoor setup you have in mind.
