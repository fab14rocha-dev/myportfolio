# Project: 4. Tantric Flyer

## What It Is
A single print flyer for **Tantric Moments** — a sensual massage business with locations in Lisbon and Albufeira, Portugal. The flyer will be placed in hotels for potential clients to pick up.

## Business Details
- **Business name:** Tantric Moments / Sensitive Touch Place, Lda.
- **Website:** https://www.tantricmoments.pt/en
- **Locations:** Lisbon and Albufeira, Portugal
- **Target audience:** Hotel guests, adults seeking sensual/wellness massage services

## What Goes on the Flyer
- Business name / logo
- List of all massages/services
- Contact information
- QR code linking to the website
- Tagline and subtle call to action

## Brand Style (from their website)
- **Colors:** Deep burgundy/wine (#8B3A3A–#A52A2A range), off-white/cream backgrounds, charcoal for text
- **Fonts:** Cinzel (headings — elegant serif), Montserrat (body — clean sans-serif)
- **Mood:** Luxury, sensual, calm, professional — think high-end spa, not explicit
- **Style:** Minimalist layouts, flowing curves, warm soft imagery, lots of white space

## Output Format
- HTML file designed to A4 print size (210mm × 297mm)
- Should look good both on screen and when printed/saved as PDF
- Keep it to one page (front of flyer)

## Stack
- Vanilla HTML + CSS only (no JS needed)
- Google Fonts for Cinzel and Montserrat
- QR code: placeholder for now, real one to be added later
